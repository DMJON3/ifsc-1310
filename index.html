    Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    My interpretation and understanding of Progressive Enhancement is that it is basically a mindset on how to properly format and create a functional website. Progressive Enchancement can come in a myriad of analogies, but they are all layered like a website should be, content->marked HTML->CSS(stylesheet)->javascript. Websites are purely made from preference wether that is your own or whomever you may be making it for, some may just want the content, others may want only the content and a small amount of CSS, and others may want everything you can throw at it and the kitchen sink!

    Now why is progressive enhancement so important? Content, and how it is displayed, that is what a website is meant to do all together, is to display a set amount of content in a visually pleasing manner.
